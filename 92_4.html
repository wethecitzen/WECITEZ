Tollywood star young tiger NTR had hosted the popular reality show Bigg Boss Telugu season one in the year 2017 and it turned out to be an instant hit. The TRPs of this show had breaking some records as well. However, the star could not take up season two as he was busy with his acting assignments. The offer then went to Nani who successfully hosted season 2.

Now, with season three on cards, there were expectations on who would be hosting the show. Well, reports are that Jr NTR is back! The star has agreed to be back on Bigg Boss for this season and has been paid a whopping Rs 2 crore for it.

In fact, when he hosted season one, Jr NTR made an astounding Rs 35 lakh per episode which was much higher than what his seniors like Chiranjeevi and Nagarjuna were paid for hosting television shows.
