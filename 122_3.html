A MiG-27 fighter plane has crashed in Jaisalmer in Rajasthan. Although the pilot managed to escape safely in the crash The incident occurred at 6.10 pm in the Pokan range. This aircraft was on the training mission. Air Force has ordered an inquiry into the accident.

This is the second Air Force Air in 12 days which has been a victim of the accident. Earlier on February 1, Miraj fighter had crashed in Bengaluru. Two pilots were killed in this accident. This aircraft was upgraded by Hindustan Aeronautics Limited and it was taken for test light.
