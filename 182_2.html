The Indian Navy is expected to release the admit cards for the common exams scheduled for the Matric Recruit (MR), Senior Secondary Recruit (SSR), and Artificer Apprentice (AA) recruitment by 6 pm on Monday. The recruitment of 146 batches is scheduled to begin from August.

The Indian Navy MR, SSR and AA examination is scheduled to be held from February 23-28, 2019. The candidates can download their AA/SSR/MR recruitment admit cards from the official website of the Indian Navy at www.joinindiannavy.gov.in.

How to download Indian Navy MR, SSR and AA 2019 admit cards:

1. Visit the website www.joinindiannavy.gov.in

2. Click on the recruitment link under the 'Current Events' tab

3. You will be redirected to a new window. Click on the 'admit card' link in the new window

4. Enter your details

5. Download your admit card and take a printout for future use

The release of the admit cards for AA/SSR/MR recruitment of the Indian Navy was earlier scheduled to be on February 11 and then later February 15. However, the date was set finally for February 18. The examination will be held to fill in vacancies to close to 3,500 posts.
