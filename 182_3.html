Bahraich (Up). On Sunday, the police arrested two Nepalese women and recovered more than two crore rupees worth of charas in the frontier Rupedha area of ​​Nepal. Police Superintendent Gaurav Grover told the police on Monday that during the search operation on the police checkpoint of the Armed Forces Force, they detained Mayaadevi (48) resident of Roppa district of Nepal and Surkumari Kammi (28) resident of Rukum district, searched them. 6 kg 400 grams of charas were recovered from both women.

He told that the value of the recovered charas in the international market is about Rs. 1 crore 92 lakhs. The arrested Nepali women smugglers have been sent to jail under the Drug Substances Act in the Siddih police station.
