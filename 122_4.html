new Delhi. A fire broke out at around 4:30 in the morning of the Hotel Arpit Palace on Gurudwara Road in Karol Bagh. 17 people died in this The fire department said that most of the deaths were caused by knee injury. Three people jumped from the fourth floor to save lives. One of them died. Two wounded. The hotel had four more floors in addition to the basement, ground floor. It is being told that the fire took place on the third floor and reached up to the top. Fire Safety Department official said that the fifth floor was being used incorrectly as a rooftop restaurant.

3 women, 1 child included in the dead A senior Delhi Police official said that the injured were taken to three hospitals including Ram Manohar Lohia. The dead include a child and three women. Eyewitnesses say the fire spreads rapidly due to wooden panels in the hotel's corridor.

35 rooms were booked by the same family It is being told that the same family had booked 35 rooms here. They came to attend a program in the city. 35 people were taken safely in the accident. The reason for the fire is not yet known. According to Sunil Chaudhary, deputy chief of fire brigade department, fire after over two and a half hours efforts was seized. Delhi's Health Minister Satyendra Jain has said that strict action will be taken against the culprits. The Collector has been ordered to investigate the case.

The top floor was sealed during the investigation, later the Rooftop restaurant was made, the Fire Department official said that during the rescue, we have got kitchens and chairs too. He said that fire safety certificate was given only for basement, first-second-third-fourth floor.

officials said that the fifth floor of the hotel was being used as a rooftop restaurant. It was made illegally. The fifth floor was sealed with brick wall. After this the hotel was given Fire Safety Certificate on December 12, 2014. After this, the Delhi Fire Service Department re-examined it on December 4, 2017. Then the hotel owner spoke of fulfilling safety rules. After that on December 24, 2017 it was certified as fit for the guest house. However, the wall used to seal the fifth floor was dropped and the Rooftop restaurant was started.
